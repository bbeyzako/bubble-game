How to Play

Controls:

  • Left Arrow: Rotate cannon left
  • Right Arrow: Rotate cannon right
  • Space / Ctrl / Up Arrow: Shoot bubble

Objective:

  • Match 3 or more bubbles of the same color
  • Clear bubbles before they reach the bottom
  • Special bubbles (stars) explode and clear nearby bubbles
